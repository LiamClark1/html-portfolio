The three movieteers!

Meet the Fockers

This is one of my favorite films, it never gets old. It's easy watching, full of fun and humour. On top of that it has a fantastic cast.

The Boat That Rocked

This film is a hidden gem, if you like the rock and roll era when pirate radio was at the forefront. Then this film will blow your socks off.

It has an awesome soundtrack, array of characters that will keep you engaged and laughing throughout.

The Matrix

If Sci-fi is your thing, you are open minded and like delving deep into a story. This action packed and thought provoking set of films is a film you can't tear yourself away from.

I truly think this is one of the most iconic films of my generation.
